Refund & Cancellation
Policy

Last updated · 28 June 2026

Clear terms for refunds, cancellations, and what happens when a project doesn't go as planned. No fine print, no surprises.

01 Cancellation by the client

You can cancel a project at any time by notifying me in writing (email is fine).

  • Before work begins: if I have not yet started development, any upfront payment is fully refunded within 14 days.
  • During development: you are invoiced for the work completed up to that point. Any prepaid amount exceeding the value of completed work is refunded. You receive all work produced so far (code, files, documentation).
  • After delivery but within the 7-day acceptance period: if the System does not materially match the agreed Project Scope and I cannot fix it within a reasonable time, you may cancel and receive a full refund.
  • After the acceptance period: the project is considered complete. Build fees are non-refundable.

02 Cancellation by the provider

If I need to cancel a project (for example, due to scope changes that make delivery unfeasible, or factors outside my control):

  • All Client materials, credentials, and any payment for work not yet completed are returned within 14 days
  • You receive all work produced up to that point

03 Refunds after delivery

Build fees are non-refundable once the 7-day acceptance period ends. This is because you own the System and its source code outright — there is nothing to "return."

04 Defects and bug fixes

Defects reported within 7 days of delivery are fixed at no additional cost. Every one-time build includes a 30-day bug-fix period. This covers things that worked during testing but broke — not new features or platform API changes (those are covered by a care plan).

05 Care plans and monthly retainers

  • Monthly care plans and retainers are billed in advance
  • You can cancel anytime — no minimum term, no penalty
  • If cancelled mid-month, the current month's service continues until the end of the billing period; unused days are not prorated
  • Cancellation takes effect from the next billing cycle

06 Disputes

If you're unhappy with a project, tell me first. Most issues are solved with direct communication. If we cannot resolve a dispute, it is governed by Romanian law as stated in the Terms & Conditions.

07 How refunds are processed

Refunds are sent via the same payment method you used (Revolut or bank transfer). Allow up to 14 days for the funds to appear, depending on your bank.
